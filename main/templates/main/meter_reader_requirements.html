Meter Reader Requirements & Tasks

Monthly Requirements Overview

As a meter reader, your primary responsibility is to collect accurate meter readings for all assigned clients on a monthly basis. This page provides you with an overview of your current tasks and requirements.

Key Requirements:
  • Collect meter readings for all assigned clients every month
  • Ensure readings are accurate and not lower than previous readings
  • Submit readings by the 5th of each month
  • Report any issues with meters or access to clients
  • Maintain professional conduct during client visits
Performance Metrics:
  • Target completion rate: 100% of assigned clients
  • Accuracy requirement: 0% negative consumption readings
  • Timeliness: All readings submitted by deadline
  • Client satisfaction: Professional and courteous service
